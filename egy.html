SUDAN

Sudan is a country located in northeastern Africa.Capital and Largest City: Khartoum is the capital and largest city of Sudan. It is also the political, cultural, and commercial center of the country. Geography: Sudan is the third-largest country in Africa, covering a vast area. It is bordered by Egypt to the north, the Red Sea to the northeast, Eritrea and Ethiopia to the east, South Sudan to the south, the Central African Republic to the southwest, Chad to the west, and Libya to the northwest.
